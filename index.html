FAQ's

Q: Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it? ▼

A: If you need to cancel or make changes, please contact us as soon as possible. Once printing has started, modifications may not be possible. However, we’ll do our best to accommodate your request if it’s within our control.

Q: How long does it take to complete an order? ▼

A: Most orders are completed within 10–15 minutes, depending on the number of pages and any custom instructions. During peak hours, there may be a slight delay, but we strive to complete all orders as quickly as possible.

Q: Is my data secure on the platform? ▼

A: Yes, data security is our top priority. All files are encrypted during upload, storage, and download. We also follow strict security protocols to ensure your documents remain private and are only accessible to you and our authorized personnel.

Q: Do you offer delivery services? ▼

A: Currently, we offer convenient in-store pickup for all orders. We’re exploring delivery options in the near future to make it even easier for you to get your documents without leaving your home or campus.

Q: What file formats are supported? ▼

A: We support popular file formats such as PDF, DOC, DOCX, JPG, and PNG. This ensures flexibility in uploading various document types for printing.

Q: Can I reprint a previous order? ▼

A: Yes, you can reprint previous orders. Simply access your order history, select the order you want to reprint, and follow the instructions. This feature is designed to save time and make reordering as convenient as possible.
